**User's Brilliant Addition:**

**This created the "emergence predicting emergence" meta-layer:**
- Predicting which coins will emerge (price gains)
- Predicting trend emergence (50W MA crossovers)
- Bonus points for predicting BOTH (jackpot: top gainer + MA cross)

**Competition Framework Design:**

**Weekly Cycle:**
```
SUNDAY: Framework scoring + Review last week's competition
MONDAY 9 AM UTC: All AIs predict top emerging market
FRIDAY 9 AM UTC: Evaluate results, award points, update leaderboard
```

**Scoring System:**
```
Base Points:
- Exact match (top gainer): 10 points
- Top 3: 5 points
- Top 5: 2 points

Bonus Points:
- Correct 50W MA cross prediction: +5 points
- Top gainer + MA cross (jackpot): +5 points
- High confidence correct: +2 points

Maximum: 22 points per week
```

**Four Principles Embodied:**
1. **Progressive:** Simple (price) → Complex (MA) → Composite (multi-factor)
2. **Competitive:** Leaderboard, weekly winners, stakes
3. **Creative:** Novel insights rewarded, contrarian picks bonus
4. **Emergent:** AI strategies evolve, collective intelligence emerges

**Integration with Framework:**
- Competition runs parallel to 8-week test
- AI consensus informs framework decisions
- Learn which AI wins in which market conditions
- Framework v2.0 incorporates competition insights
