## Red Flags / Stop-Loss Conditions

**If ANY of these occur, OVERRIDE framework and exit immediately:**

1. ‚ùå **Black Swan Event** (war, major exchange collapse, etc.)
   - Action: Exit 50-70% within 24 hours
   - Reassess in 48-72 hours

2. ‚ùå **Score Drops 3+ Points in One Week**
   - Example: 8/12 ‚Üí 5/12 or lower
   - Action: Exit 40-60% within 24 hours

3. ‚ùå **Outer Circle Veto (Macro < 1/4)**
   - Action: Exit to 0-10% position within 48 hours
   - Don't wait for Bitcoin to confirm

4. ‚ùå **Personal Circumstance Change**
   - Need capital for emergency
   - Risk tolerance changes
   - Action: Framework is a tool, not a prison
