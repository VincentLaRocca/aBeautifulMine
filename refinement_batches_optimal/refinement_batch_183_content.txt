2. Bearish Divergence:
   - Occurs when the price makes a higher high, but the technical indicator makes a lower high, indicating a potential bearish reversal.
   - Traders may look to enter short positions or sell the rally when a bearish divergence is identified.

3. Hidden Divergence:
   - A more complex divergence pattern where the price and indicator move in the same direction, but the strength of the moves differs.
