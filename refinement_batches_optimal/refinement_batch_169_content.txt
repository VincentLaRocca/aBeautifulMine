   - Providing liquidity across multiple AMM pools with different token pairs can reduce overall impermanent loss exposure.
   - This diversification strategy helps offset losses in one pool with gains in another.
   - Sources: Messari research on multi-pool liquidity mining, DeFi Pulse article on diversified LPs.

   - Deploying liquidity only when market conditions are favorable can limit impermanent loss.
   - This involves using price oracles and trading signals to determine optimal windows for providing liquidity.
   - Sources: Bancor blog post on conditional liquidity, research paper on automated market maker design.

   - Employing hedging techniques like options or futures contracts can help offset impermanent loss.
