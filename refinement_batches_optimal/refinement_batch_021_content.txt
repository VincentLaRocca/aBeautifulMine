   - In thinly traded or highly volatile cryptocurrency markets, the ADL may be more susceptible to manipulation by large traders or market makers.

   - The ADL is primarily used for analyzing individual assets or trading pairs, rather than providing a comprehensive view of the overall cryptocurrency market.
