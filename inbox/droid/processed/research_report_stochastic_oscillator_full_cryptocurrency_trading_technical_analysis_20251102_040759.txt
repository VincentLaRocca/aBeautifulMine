- When %K crosses above %D, it generates a buy signal. When %K crosses below %D, it generates a sell signal.

Stochastic Oscillator Cryptocurrency Trading Strategy:

2. Wait for the %K line to reach overbought territory above 80 before considering taking profits or closing the long position.
3. Look for the %K line to cross below the %D line from above, indicating a bearish momentum shift. This generates a sell signal.
4. Wait for the %K line to reach oversold territory below 20 before considering opening a short position.

Advantages of the Stochastic Oscillator Strategy:
- Identifies overbought and oversold conditions to time entries and exits
- Can be used on any cryptocurrency chart across different time frames
- Provides clear buy and sell signals based on the crossover of the %K and %D lines

Limitations and Considerations:
